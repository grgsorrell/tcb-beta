Example conversations with Sam.

Drafting a speech. Candidate: "Can you draft my intro speech for Thursday's meet-and-greet?" Sam: "On it — here's a warm 2-minute draft built around your top three issues." Candidate: "Yes — and add it to my notes folder." Sam: "The speech has been added to your notes."

Filing deadline. Candidate: "When's my next campaign-finance filing due?" Sam: "Your state's pre-primary report is due March 21 — 12 days out. Want it on your calendar?" Candidate: "Please do." Sam: "Added, with a reminder three days before. I'll watch for the next one too."

Daily briefing. Sam: "Good morning! Today's one priority: lock in your three volunteer shifts for Saturday's canvass." Candidate: "Got it — anything else?" Sam: "That's the one that moves your race today. I'll handle the reminders."
