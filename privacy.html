Last updated: June 9, 2026  ·  Effective date: June 9, 2026

In short. We collect the information you give us and a limited amount of technical data needed to run the Service. We use it to operate The Candidate's Toolbox, process payments, and improve the product. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your content to train third-party AI models. 1Who we are & scope

This Privacy Policy explains how The Candidate's Toolbox ("TCB," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you use our website, applications, the AI assistant "Sam," and related services (the "Service").

This Policy is incorporated into our Terms of Service. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, do not use the Service.

2Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information — your username, email address, password (stored only in hashed form), and account role.
  • Campaign profile — details you enter about your candidacy, office, district, jurisdiction, election dates, budget, opponents, endorsements, and similar campaign data.
  • Your Content — documents, notes, tasks, calendar entries, and the messages you exchange with Sam.
  • Communications — information you provide when you contact us for support or feedback.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage and device data — IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, and feature usage, collected to operate and secure the Service.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — used for authentication (keeping you logged in) and core functionality. See Section 9.

Payment information

When you subscribe, payment is processed by our third-party payment processor, Stripe. Stripe collects your payment-card and billing details directly. We do not receive or store your full payment-card number. We receive limited information such as the last four digits of your card, card brand, billing status, and subscription state. Stripe's handling of your data is governed by Stripe's own privacy policy.

3How we use information

We use personal information to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and secure the Service, including authenticating you and isolating your workspace from other users;
  • Generate AI assistance through Sam based on your inputs and campaign profile;
  • Process subscriptions, billing, and renewals through our payment processor;
  • Respond to your support requests and communicate with you about the Service, including service and security notices;
  • Monitor, analyze, and improve the Service, including diagnosing problems and developing new features;
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, security incidents, and violations of our Terms;
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

4AI processing of your information

To generate responses, the Service sends relevant portions of your inputs and campaign data to AI model providers that process it to produce output for you. We take steps to limit what is shared to what is needed to provide the feature.

We do not use Your Content to train third-party foundation models, and we do not sell Your Content. We may use aggregated or de-identified data — which does not identify you — to understand usage and improve the Service. AI output may be inaccurate; how to treat it is described in our Terms of Service.

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as described below:

  • Service providers — vendors who help us operate the Service, including our payment processor (Stripe), hosting and cloud infrastructure, AI model providers, and search/research infrastructure. They may process personal information only to provide services to us and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Within your workspace — if you are a sub-user, your account owner can access activity and content within their workspace. Account owners are responsible for how they handle their sub-users' information.
  • Legal and safety — when we believe disclosure is required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of TCB, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.

6Data retention & security

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When you cancel and request deletion, we delete or de-identify your personal information within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as fraud prevention and recordkeeping.

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information, including encryption in transit, hashed passwords, and access controls that isolate each workspace. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through the options on our website. We will verify your request before acting on it and will respond within the time required by applicable law. You may use an authorized agent where the law permits.
